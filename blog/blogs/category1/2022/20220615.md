---
title: vue3与vue2中使用keepAlive动态缓存组件
date: 2022-06-15
tags:
 - VUE
categories:
 - VUE
---

> vue3
> App.vue

```js
<router-view v-slot="{ Component }">
  <keep-alive>
    <component :is="Component" :key="route.name" v-if="route.meta.keepAlive" />
  </keep-alive>
  <component :is="Component" :key="route.name" v-if="!route.meta.keepAlive" />
</router-view>
```

> vue2
> App.vue

```js
<keep-alive>
  <router-view v-if="$route.meta.keepAlive"></router-view>
</keep-alive>
<router-view v-if="!$route.meta.keepAlive"></router-view>
```
